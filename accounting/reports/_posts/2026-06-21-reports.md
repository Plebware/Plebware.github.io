# 📊 Financial Reports

Financial reports turn accounting data into useful information.

They help individuals, businesses, and organisations understand:

- Where money comes from
- Where money goes
- What is owned
- What is owed
- How financially healthy things are

A report transforms numbers into knowledge.

---

# 🔑 What Are Financial Reports?

A financial report is a structured summary of financial activity.

Reports help answer questions like:

> "Are we growing, losing money, or staying balanced?"

They support:

- Decision making
- Planning
- Budgeting
- Tax preparation
- Business management

---

# 📚 Common Accounting Reports

## 💰 Income Statement

Also called a:

- Profit and Loss Report
- P&L

It shows:

- Income
- Expenses
- Profit
- Loss

It answers:

> "Did we earn more than we spent?"

---

## 🏦 Balance Sheet

A balance sheet shows financial position.

It lists:

### Assets

Things you own:

- Cash
- Equipment
- Property
- Investments

### Liabilities

Things you owe:

- Loans
- Bills
- Debts

### Equity

The value remaining after liabilities are removed.

---

## 💵 Cash Flow Report

Cash flow tracks movement of money.

It shows:

- Money coming in
- Money going out
- Available cash

Cash flow helps prevent financial surprises.

---

# 📅 Budget Reports

Budget reports compare:

- Planned spending
- Actual spending

They help identify:

- Overspending
- Savings opportunities
- Future needs

---

# 🧾 Tax Reports

Tax reports organise information needed for taxation.

They may include:

- Income summaries
- Expense records
- Tax calculations
- Payment history

Good reporting makes tax preparation easier.

---

# 📈 Business Performance Reports

Businesses use reports to measure:

- Sales
- Costs
- Growth
- Productivity
- Profit margins

These reports help guide decisions.

---

# 🛠️ Reporting Tools

Financial reports can be created using:

- Spreadsheets
- Accounting software
- Databases
- Business intelligence tools

- GNUCash

- Accounting platforms

---

# 🌍 Why Reports Matter

Without reports, financial information becomes a collection of disconnected numbers.

Reports create a clear picture.

They help people:

- Make informed choices
- Plan ahead
- Track progress
- Stay organised

---

# 🔑 Key Points

1. Reports turn financial data into useful information.
2. Different reports answer different questions.
3. Good reporting supports better decisions.
