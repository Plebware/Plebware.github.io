# 🔑 **100 Subcategories Is Not Too Much.**

## 🏗️ A Response To The Critics

A critique aimed at **PlebWare** is that it is too ambitious.

It was said that I have created too many subcategories.

> “What a lot of Tommy-rot”

## 🌱 What Is Ordained By Nature

What is ordained by nature, should be rather expressed as what is right!

Or even better:

🙏 What is ordained by God, is always the right thing.

## 📚 Books, Knowledge, And Curiosity

I have read in books, and books are a good reference point.

I have also been in the computer game for a very long time.

Yes, I am terribly eccentric, and as is recommended:

> One should write about that which interests you.

The bitter pill is this:

I have very many interests, and it is not by accident.

## 👨‍👦 The Foundation Of Knowledge

For you see, I had an earthly daddy, who invested a lot of money and effort in ensuring I had a good education.

For starters, he built a wall-sized bookshelf in our lounge, and filled it with:

📚 Encyclopedias  
🔬 Science books  
⚙️ Technical books  
📖 Various other literary works  

That foundation shaped the way I think.

## 🧠 Many Interests, One Purpose

So even though there are many topics, this will be no strenuous task for me.

For starters, I see myself as an Irish Terrier (Tenacious).

Although I hate multitasking, I am pretty good at it.

For example, while compiling this explainer in the most civil manner possible, I am also:

⛳ Collecting Golf Clash chests for Juelz  
🎧 Listening to an audiobook  
📖 Enjoying Selected Works of E.M. Forster — *The Longest Journey*  

What I am saying is:

Although there are 100 subcategories, I am more than capable, and I love writing, in a graduated manner.

## ✍️ My Writing Style

Do I have a fixed style of writing?

Not exactly.

But I enjoy writing with:

😏 Wit  
⚡ Tempestuous sarcasm  

Especially now that I leverage AI to improve my written work.

Especially now, at six-point-three decades of life experience, and having discovered that my purpose is to teach others to achieve.

## ⌨️ The Keyboard Is Mightier Than The Pen

With all that said, my motto:

> **"The Keyboard Is Mightier Than The Pen"**

is not just a silly marketing ploy I thought up twenty-three years ago.

Now is my time to prove it.

## 🔥 A Mind In Motion

There is not a muscle in my mind that remains idle when I am seated behind that proverbial keyboard.

Be it:

📱 The Android virtual keyboard of AI Type  
⌨️ The full QWERTY layout on my laptop  

From which this masterpiece is being produced.

## ⚙️ The Engineer's Mind

Fallaciously I might be at times, but picturesque speech is not beyond my grasp.

I might be scientific and philosophical.

My years of working as a systems analyst, or rather that of working as an Electrical Fitter for the South African Railways, has embedded that style of thinking in my mind.

That is why there are products such as:

⚙️ **PlebMachine**

And for that I most certainly will not apologise.

## 🚀 The Journey Continues

I can assure you all of one thing:

This endeavour, project, or whatever you want to call it:

**Will not fail.**

Nor will it go away.

It has been two decades in the making.

And I will see it through to the end.

🔑
