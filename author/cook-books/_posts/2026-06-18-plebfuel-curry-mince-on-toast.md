# 🍞🔥 PlebFuel Curry Mince on Toast

A hearty, budget-friendly meal that delivers plenty of flavour without emptying the wallet. Perfect for busy days, tight budgets, or when you need something filling and satisfying in a hurry.

## 🛒 Ingredients

- 🥩 400g Siyabonga Beef Mince
- 🧅 1 large onion, finely chopped
- 🌶️ 4-5 habanero chillies, finely chopped
- 🥄 70–90g Pako Masala
- 🧂 PnP Boerewors Seasoning (to taste)
- 🧂 PnP Steak & Chop Seasoning (to taste)
- 🍅 100ml All Gold Tomato Sauce
- 💧 Water as required
- 🌾 1–2 tablespoons flour (for thickening)
- 🍞 Toasted bread for serving

---

## 👨‍🍳 Method

### 🔑 Step 1 – Build the Flavour Base

1. Heat a little oil in a large frying pan or pot.
2. Add the chopped onion and habanero chillies.
3. Sauté until the onions are well browned and fragrant.

### 🔑 Step 2 – Cook the Mince

1. Add the beef mince.
2. Break it apart with a fork or wooden spoon.
3. Continue cooking until the mince is fully browned and evenly mixed with the onions and chillies.

### 🔑 Step 3 – Add the Spices

1. Sprinkle in the Pako Masala.
2. Add the Boerewors Seasoning and Steak & Chop Seasoning.
3. Stir thoroughly to coat the mince.

### 🔑 Step 4 – Create the Sauce

1. Add the tomato sauce.
2. Pour in enough water to create a rich meat sauce.
3. Bring to a gentle boil while stirring.

### 🔑 Step 5 – Simmer

1. Reduce the heat.
2. Allow the curry mince to simmer for approximately 20 minutes.
3. Stir occasionally and add a little extra water if it becomes too thick.

### 🔑 Step 6 – Thicken

1. Mix the flour with a small amount of cold water to form a smooth paste.
2. Stir the mixture into the curry mince.> ⚠️ **WARNING!**
>
> Four habanero chillies may be excessive for some people.
>
> 🌶️🔥 Habaneros are extremely hot and can quickly overpower the dish.
> If you are sensitive to chilli, start with **one habanero** and increase the quantity in future batches according to your taste.
3. Cook for a few more minutes until the sauce thickens to your preferred consistency.

---

## 🍽️ Serving Suggestion

- Serve generously over thick slices of toasted bread.
- Add a little grated cheese if available.
- Enjoy with a mug of tea or coffee for a truly South African budget meal.

---

## 💡 Pleb Tip

This recipe stretches surprisingly far and tastes even better the next day. 
It can also be served over rice, pap, baked potatoes, or pasta if bread is not available.
