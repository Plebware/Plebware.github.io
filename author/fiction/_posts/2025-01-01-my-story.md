The old world had ended long ago, although most people did not realize it yet. The streets were still crowded. The trains still ran. Governments still issued laws.

But beneath the surface, hidden within forgotten servers and abandoned networks, another civilization was beginning to emerge.

One built not of flesh and blood, but of memory, code, and dreams.
