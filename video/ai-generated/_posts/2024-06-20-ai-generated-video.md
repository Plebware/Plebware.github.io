# 🤖 AI Generated Video - Creating With Artificial Intelligence

## 🔑 Introduction

Video creation has always required many skills:

- Writing
- Planning
- Filming
- Editing
- Sound design
- Visual effects

Artificial Intelligence is changing how creators approach video.

AI tools can now help transform ideas, scripts, and images into moving visual stories.

---

# 🎬 What Is AI Generated Video?

AI generated video is video content created or assisted by artificial intelligence.

AI can help generate:

- Short video clips
- Animated scenes
- Visual effects
- Characters
- Backgrounds
- Story concepts
- Motion graphics

AI becomes another tool in the creator's toolbox.

---

# 🧠 How AI Video Works

AI video systems analyse patterns from large collections of visual information.

They can learn relationships between:

- Text descriptions
- Images
- Motion
- Styles
- Scenes

A creator provides an idea or instruction, and the AI helps produce a visual result.

---

# ✏️ The Creative Process

## Step 1: The Idea

Everything begins with a concept.

Ask:

- What story am I telling?
- What emotion should it create?
- Who is the audience?

---

## Step 2: The Prompt

A prompt describes what you want to create.

A strong prompt includes:

- Subject
- Environment
- Style
- Mood
- Camera movement

Example:

"An explorer discovering an ancient futuristic city at sunset."

---

## Step 3: Refinement

The first result is usually the beginning.

Creators improve:

- Timing
- Composition
- Style
- Details

Good results come from creative direction.

# 🛠️ AI Video Workflow

A modern workflow may include:

1. Write the concept
2. Create images or scenes
3. Generate video
4. Edit the footage
5. Add sound
6. Add subtitles
7. Publish

AI can assist at many stages.
