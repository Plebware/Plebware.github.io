# 🎧 Audacity - Open Source Audio Editing For Everyone

## 🔑 Introduction

Before modern AI tools, online studios, and advanced production platforms became common, many creators started with simple tools that made creativity possible.

One of those tools was **Audacity**.

For many beginners, Audacity became their first step into:

- Audio recording
- Music editing
- Podcast creation
- Sound design
- Digital storytelling

It proved that professional creativity did not have to begin with expensive equipment.

---

# 🎵 What Is Audacity?

Audacity is a free, open-source audio editor.

It allows creators to:

- Record audio
- Edit sound
- Mix tracks
- Remove unwanted noise
- Add effects
- Export audio files

It became popular because it was accessible to everyone.
