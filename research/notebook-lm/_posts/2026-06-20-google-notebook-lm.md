# 🔑 What Is NotebookLM?

NotebookLM is an AI-powered research and note-taking tool designed to help people understand, organize, and work with information.

Instead of searching the entire internet for answers, NotebookLM focuses on the sources you provide.

It helps you:

* Read documents
* Summarize information
* Ask questions about your material
* Create study notes
* Organize research
* Discover connections between ideas

Think of it as a digital study partner that works with your own documents.

The basic process is simple:

## Step 1: Add Your Sources
