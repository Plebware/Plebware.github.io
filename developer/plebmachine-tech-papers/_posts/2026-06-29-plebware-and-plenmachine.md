# 🔑 **Two Connected Projects**

## PlebWare — Personal Knowledge Publishing Platform

PlebWare is a publishing environment built around structured Markdown content.

Its purpose is to create, organise, preserve, and share knowledge.

The platform focuses on:

- Writing
- Documentation
- Tutorials
- Research
- Creative works
- Educational material

PlebWare is the knowledge layer.

---

## PlebMachine — Modular Software Environment

PlebMachine is a software project designed around modular desktop computing.

Its purpose is to create a flexible user-focused computing environment where tools, workflows, and creative processes can be organised into a unified system.

PlebMachine is the technology layer.

---

## The Connection

At PlebMachine, we provide the environment where knowledge creation can happen.

PlebWare provides the platform where the resulting knowledge can be published.

Together:

PlebMachine → Creates the environment  
PlebWare → Publishes the knowledge
